What is Urban Planning

Urban or city planning is the process of designing and managing how physical, social and economic development occurs in a built environment like a city, town, etc. City planning attempts to predict and shape how a city will develop in the future, basing a lot of what is done or planned on a variety of aspects, such as:

  • Trying to Anticipate how the city will look or function in the future.
  • Assessing the current and trying to predict potential needs in a city.
  • The change of demographics in a city over time and how that difference in demographics, between now and then could impact the development and other factor in the city.

The goal of city planning is to ensure that a city which is a continually evolving environment can develop in an organized and sustainable manner. Overtime shaping patterns of community growth and the overall quality of life in a city
